Sunday, August 30, 2015 the Ministry of Agriculture signed a memorandum of understanding in the field of breeding shrimp and fish aquaculture in Saudi Arabia Saudi Aquaculture was signed by Undersecretary of the Ministry of Agriculture for Fisheries Monthly Engineer Jaber Mohammed today, a memorandum of understanding with one of the specialized in aquaculture companies that target testing for shrimp farming Alvanama fish Albaovlok system in various regions of the Kingdom environments conditions. The ministry is working to provide innovative solutions to the aquaculture sector in the Kingdom to ensure sustainability, productivity and environmental safety at the same time; it was the ministry signed a memorandum on the basis of preliminary studies which has Fisheries Jeddah research which proved the success of the experiment at the research level, as well as the existence of signs of success on a commercial scale and access center energy production according to plan for the development of sustainable aquaculture industry in the Kingdom to achieve target levels. The ministry is working according to a clear mechanisms for joint action with the private sector in respect of fisheries areas through the Saudi Society for aquaculture. The ministry recently received a special invitation from the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO) to participate in the conference to be held during the month of October 2015 for aquaculture in Brazil, which is also aimed at fine-tuning expertise Kingdom in promoting the areas of cooperation between the public and private sectors with the rest of the states, This conference will bring about 120 countries from all over the world.
